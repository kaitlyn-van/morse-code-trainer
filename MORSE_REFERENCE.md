## ⏱️ Timing Guide
| Input | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Dot · | Short press (< 200ms) |
| Dash − | Long press (≥ 200ms) |
| Letter gap | Pause for 800ms after last symbol |
| New word | Automatically loads after correct/incorrect submission |
